What is Goal Goal (GG) or Both Teams To Score (BTTS)?

BTTS/GG simply means that both teams in a football match will score at least one goal or goals each. This betting market has two outcomes: Yes and No. A Yes outcome means that both teams will score at least one goal each, while a No outcome means that at least one of the teams will fail to score.
